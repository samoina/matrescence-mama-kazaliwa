Journey to Matrescence...

I remember vividly when I discovered I was pregnant in 2016. I had mixed feelings of extremely scared but yes very excited about what the new chapter in my life would hold. I was always looking forward to the day I would become a mother. I still remember vividly the day my daughter came, I was so scared of how my body would handle the pain and soon after that my reality came in. I was struggling and all these big feelings started trickling in.
Shame, guilt, doubt and I felt like I could not find a way to express what it was I was feeling. It was beautiful, don't get me wrong to watch the creation that was once in my body in my arms but there was something missing and I did not know what it was yet. When I found out about matrescence, It was like the aha! moment where everything began to make sense, I could finally begin to understand what a mother goes through and that a mother is born and experiences similar changes just like the baby. I wasn't crazy after all and this led me to a series of reaching out to other moms and sharing my own naked truths.

Mama Kazaliwa Community

Motherhood is natural, not automatic.
Motherhood looks different for each and every mom. Mama Kazaliwa is here to support women during this transition. Simply put, Matrescence is the process of becoming a mother, and it embodies the significant physical, emotional and psychological changes. Mama Kazaliwa is here to support you as you navigate this identity change, meeting you where you are with grace and kindness.
